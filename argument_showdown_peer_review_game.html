How to play
  1. Divide into four teams. Each team gets a copy of the same student argumentative draft.
  2. Teams read the essay silently for 5 minutes before play begins.
  3. Teams take turns selecting a category and point value from the board above.
  4. The instructor reads the prompt aloud. Teams write their answer (30 seconds), then share out.
  5. Best answer — most specific, most textually grounded — earns the points.
  6. Ties go to the team that can name the exact location in the essay (paragraph number, line).
  7. Play until the board is cleared. Highest score wins.
